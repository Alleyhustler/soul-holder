Human Traffic
Fandom: Death Note Character or Pairing Focus: Mello x Near
Mello faces a dilemma, and Near leaves him with some interesting memories. While completing his book detailing the Los Angeles BB murder cases, Mello pauses to write about his beguiling encounter with Near.

Some thoughts about this piece over a decade later: I really dislike this one. There's a strong central emotive idea here (namely: Near lives his life as a solitary, isolated individual, but still perceives himself as part of a wider, interconnected world, and his tenuous friendship with Mello is his main connection to all of that) but it's conveyed with the elegance and subtlety of a hammer to the face. Mello is canonically a writer said to have an informal, casual style of writing, so I tried to channel that here.
